Quick Start

A simple order that keeps the app tidy from day one.

  1. Set up your filter groups in Settings. Use things like Recipe Type, Season, and Effort.
  2. Add a few aisle categories that match how you walk through your supermarket.
  3. Create recipes with the right ingredients, default servings, and tags.
  4. Fill the two-week planner with recipes or simple manual meals.
  5. Review the grocery list and add any last manual items before shopping.
